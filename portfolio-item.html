Frogger

GA SEI Project 1

For my first project in the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive Course, i was tasked to make grid-based game inspired by the classic arcade title Frogger, utilising the JavaScript skills i have acquired up to this point.

Frogger is a grid-based game where the player, a frog, starts at the bottom of the grid, and must travel to the top of the grid to beat the game. The player must pass two obstacles before reaching the goal:
1. The first obstacle is a road in which cars will be travelling left and right. The player must cross the road while avoiding the cars.
2. The second obstacle is a river, where there will be logs floating left and right. The player must cross the river while avoiding the water. This will act as the reverse of the first obstacle - instead of avoiding the logs the player must get on them to cross to the other side and reach the goal.
